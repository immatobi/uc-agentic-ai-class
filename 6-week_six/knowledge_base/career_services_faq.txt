CAREER SERVICES FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

GENERAL SERVICES

Q: What services are offered?
A: Resume reviews, career coaching, internship support, mock interviews, networking events, employer presentations, and job search assistance.

Q: Are services free?
A: Yes, all currently enrolled students may access services at no additional cost.

RESUME SUPPORT

Q: How do I schedule a resume review?
A: Through the Career Services Portal.

Q: How long are appointments?
A: Standard appointments are 30 minutes.

INTERNSHIPS

Q: Does Career Services help students find internships?
A: Yes. Opportunities are posted on the Career Portal and shared through employer partnerships.

Q: Are internships required?
A: Requirements vary by academic program.

MOCK INTERVIEWS

Q: Can I practice interviews?
A: Yes. Students can schedule virtual or in-person mock interviews.

CAREER FAIRS

Q: How often are career fairs held?
A: The university hosts Fall and Spring Career Fairs annually.

EMPLOYER EVENTS

Q: Do employers visit campus?
A: Yes. Employers regularly conduct information sessions and recruiting events.

GRADUATE SCHOOL SUPPORT

Q: Is graduate school advising available?
A: Yes. Advisors assist with application strategies, personal statements, and program selection.

ALUMNI SERVICES

Q: Can recent graduates access services?
A: Alumni may use Career Services for up to one year after graduation.

JOB SEARCH STRATEGIES

Career Services recommends:
- Networking
- Tailored resumes
- LinkedIn optimization
- Interview preparation
- Consistent application tracking
