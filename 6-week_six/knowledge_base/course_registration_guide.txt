COURSE REGISTRATION GUIDE
Academic Year 2026–2027

OVERVIEW

The registration process allows students to enroll in courses required for degree completion. Students are responsible for ensuring registration accuracy and compliance with academic policies.

REGISTRATION PERIODS

Fall Semester:
April 1 – August 20

Spring Semester:
October 1 – January 15

Summer Semester:
February 1 – May 20

REGISTRATION STEPS

1. Meet with academic advisor.
2. Review degree requirements.
3. Check course availability.
4. Verify prerequisite completion.
5. Register through the Student Portal.
6. Confirm schedule and tuition assessment.

PREREQUISITES

Certain courses require completion of prerequisite courses. Registration may be blocked if prerequisites are not satisfied.

WAITLISTS

Students may join waitlists when courses are full.
Seats are offered in waitlist order.
Students have 24 hours to accept an offered seat.

MAXIMUM COURSE LOAD

Undergraduate:
18 credit hours per semester.

Graduate:
12 credit hours per semester.

Students seeking overload approval must obtain department authorization.

ADD/DROP PERIOD

Students may add or drop courses during the first two weeks of each semester.

WITHDRAWALS

Course withdrawals after the add/drop period will appear on transcripts as a W grade.

REGISTRATION HOLDS

Students with academic, disciplinary, or financial holds may be prevented from registering.

ONLINE COURSES

Online courses follow the same registration procedures as in-person courses.

SPECIAL TOPICS COURSES

Department approval may be required for enrollment.

AUDITING COURSES

Students may audit selected courses with instructor approval. Audited courses do not count toward degree requirements.

COMMON ISSUES

Issue: Course Full
Resolution: Join waitlist.

Issue: Missing Prerequisite
Resolution: Contact department for review.

Issue: Registration Hold
Resolution: Resolve hold with appropriate office.
