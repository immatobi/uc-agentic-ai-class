UNIVERSITY TUITION AND FEES GUIDE

Version 2026

TUITION

Undergraduate Tuition:
$450 per credit hour

Graduate Tuition:
$650 per credit hour

Students enrolled full-time are charged based on program requirements.

PAYMENT DEADLINES

Fall Semester:
Payment due August 15

Spring Semester:
Payment due January 10

Summer Semester:
Payment due May 15

Students who fail to make payment by the deadline may incur late fees.

LATE FEES

A late fee of $100 will be applied to unpaid balances after the payment deadline.

Accounts that remain unpaid for more than 60 days may be placed on financial hold.

FINANCIAL HOLDS

Students with financial holds may be unable to:

- Register for classes
- Receive transcripts
- Graduate

SCHOLARSHIPS

Merit Scholarship:
Awarded to students with a GPA of 3.7 or higher.

Leadership Scholarship:
Awarded to students demonstrating exceptional leadership and community involvement.

Need-Based Scholarship:
Available to students who demonstrate financial need.

REFUND POLICY

Students who withdraw during the first two weeks of the semester are eligible for a 100% tuition refund.

Students who withdraw during weeks three and four are eligible for a 50% tuition refund.

No refunds are provided after the fourth week of classes.

PAYMENT METHODS

Accepted payment methods include:

- Credit card
- Debit card
- Bank transfer
- Certified check

INSTALLMENT PLANS

Students may enroll in a monthly payment plan.

A $50 administrative fee applies to all installment plans.

INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

International students must settle all tuition and fees before course registration is finalized.
