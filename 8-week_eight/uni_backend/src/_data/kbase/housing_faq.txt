UNIVERSITY HOUSING FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Version 2026

GENERAL INFORMATION

Q: What housing options are available?
A: The university offers traditional residence halls, suite-style housing, apartment-style housing, and graduate student housing.

Q: Who is eligible for on-campus housing?
A: Full-time undergraduate, graduate, and international students are eligible. Priority is given to first-year students and international students.

APPLICATION PROCESS

Q: How do I apply for housing?
A: Students must complete the online Housing Application through the Student Portal. Applications open on March 1 and close on July 1 for Fall admission.

Q: Is there an application fee?
A: Yes. A non-refundable housing application fee of $75 is required.

Q: Can I change my housing preferences after submission?
A: Students may update preferences until the application deadline.

ROOM ASSIGNMENTS

Q: How are room assignments made?
A: Assignments are based on application date, student classification, accommodation needs, and housing availability.

Q: Can I request a roommate?
A: Yes. Both students must mutually request each other before the assignment deadline.

Q: When will assignments be released?
A: Housing assignments are typically released by August 1.

MEAL PLANS

Q: Are meal plans mandatory?
A: Students living in traditional residence halls must purchase a meal plan.

Q: What meal plan options exist?
A: Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Unlimited plans are available.

CHECK-IN AND MOVE-IN

Q: When can students move in?
A: Fall move-in begins one week before classes start.

Q: What documents are required?
A: Students must present a photo ID and housing confirmation.

POLICIES

Q: Are pets allowed?
A: Only approved service animals are permitted.

Q: Is smoking allowed?
A: All university housing is smoke-free.

Q: Are guests permitted?
A: Overnight guests are allowed for up to three consecutive nights with roommate approval.

MAINTENANCE

Q: How do I report maintenance issues?
A: Requests should be submitted through the Housing Portal.

Q: What is the average response time?
A: Emergency requests are handled within 24 hours. Non-emergency requests typically within 3–5 business days.

HOUSING CANCELLATIONS

Q: Can I cancel housing?
A: Yes, but cancellation fees may apply depending on timing.

Q: What are cancellation fees?
A: Before July 1: no fee. July 1–August 1: $250. After move-in: prorated charges may apply.
